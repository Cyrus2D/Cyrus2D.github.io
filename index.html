Introduction

Cyrus is a soccer simulation 2d team that established in 2011 by Shiraz University of Technology, We participated in RoboCup since 2013, IranOpen since 2012, Cyrus was in second place in RoboCup 2018 and third place in RoboCup 2019 and was champion of IranOpen soccer simulation 2d team in 2014, 2017, 2018.
